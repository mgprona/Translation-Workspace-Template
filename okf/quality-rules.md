# Quality Rules

- Do not swap older brother and younger brother.
- Do not leave stray English, Korean, or edit marks in the prose.
- Do not add translator notes inside the narrative.
- Do not change the meaning of a title just to make it sound fancy.
- Do not use two Thai renderings for the same proper noun.
- Do not forget the source hierarchy.
- Do not let chapter markup leak into the final published title.
- Do not make the story feel modern when the source is wuxia-fantasy.
